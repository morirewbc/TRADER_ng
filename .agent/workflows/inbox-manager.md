---
description: Manage Gmail inbox for triaging, summarizing, drafting, and organizing
---
1. Triage & Scan: Search for unread messages in INBOX, group into Critical, Calendar/Events, Updates/Newsletters, and Promo, and summarize for the user.
2. Auto-Archive: Identify low-priority automated emails, confirm with the user, and use gmail.modify to remove the INBOX label.
3. Drafting Responses: Read the full thread context, use gmail.createDraft to write a professional response, and output the draft ID, subject, and summary to the user.
4. Strictly follow Decision Rules: Never permanently delete emails unless told, always summarize long threads before decisions, respect VIP lists.

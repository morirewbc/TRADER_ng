# NGX Stock Tickers Reference

Nigerian Exchange Group (NGX) stock tickers for use in TradingView PineScript.
Always prefix with `NGX:` when using `request.security()` or as a symbol string.

## NGX:DANGCEM
**Dangote Cement** — Largest cement producer in sub-Saharan Africa. Headquarters Lagos.
- Sector: Industrial Goods
- Indices: NGX30, NGSEINDEX, NGXIND
- Usage: `request.security("NGX:DANGCEM", timeframe.period, close)`
- Keywords: dangote, cement, industrial, construction, building materials

# NGX Market Indices

## NGX:NGSEINDEX
**NGX All Share Index** — Benchmark index tracking all listed equities on the Nigerian Exchange.
- Type: Broad market index
- Usage: `request.security("NGX:NGSEINDEX", timeframe.period, close)`
- Keywords: ngx all share, nse index, benchmark, market index, nigeria stock market

## NGX:NGX30
**NGX 30 Index** — Tracks 30 most capitalised and liquid stocks on NGX.
- Type: Blue-chip index
- Components include: DANGCEM, MTNN, ZENITHBANK, GTCO, ACCESS, AIRTELAFRI, UBA, BUACEMENT, BUAFOODS, SEPLAT and others
- Usage: `request.security("NGX:NGX30", timeframe.period, close)`
- Keywords: ngx 30, blue chip, top stocks, large cap, market index
